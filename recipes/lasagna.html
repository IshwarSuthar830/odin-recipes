Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made from layers of wide, flat pasta sheets, alternating with rich fillings. Traditionally, it features a savory mixture of ground meat (such as beef or pork), tomato-based sauce, creamy bechamel, and a variety of cheeses, usually mozzarella, Parmesan, and ricotta. The layers are baked together until the top is golden and bubbly, creating a hearty, flavorful casserole with a comforting, melt-in-your-mouth texture. Lasagna can be customized with vegetables, different meats, or even made vegetarian, making it a versatile favorite in both casual and special meals.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Tomato
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Cheese
  • Egg
  • Lasagna Noodles

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
